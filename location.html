Location & Amenities

We wanted our wedding to be a special and a fun experience rather than just a few events on separate nights. We also wanted people to come together at a place where they can not only be a part of our wedding but also enjoy a great stress free vacation. We think Cancun and Moon Palace are a great combination to truly have a memorable wedding weekend with our family and friends.



A short 10 minutes from Cancun International Airport and 15 minutes from Cancun Island, the Moon Palace is ideally located on Cancun's tranquil south shore, far enough away from other resorts that you can enjoy a measure of privacy, yet close enough to all the action you desire.


Moon Palace offers unparalleled service that will set the stage for an exceptional experience throughout the duration your stay. Spacious accommodations feature luxurious amenities including double whirlpool tub in every room, 24-hour room service, included unlimited long distance phone calls to the continental US and Canada, wireless Internet and brand name liquor dispensers. 
Extensive offering of All-Inclusive Features... includes all of these services, amenities and activities: 

Luxurious Accommodations

Inspiring views, a double whirlpool tub in every room, 24-hour room service, luxurious décor including a marble bathroom with glass-enclosed shower and a private balcony. Moon Palace’s abundant in-room amenities will surround you in comfort and elegance, from nightly turndown service to plush bathrobes and slippers to Farouk bath amenities.

Fine Dining

With 14 exceptional restaurants, you can dine at a different place every night of the week and enjoy a variety of international cuisine, including Mexican, American, Italian and Asian fare. Quench your thirst at any of our twelve bars, including six in the pool. No restrictions on buffet or a la carte restaurants. Reservation required for La Trattoria & Bambu. Unlimited wine by the bottle from wineries from around the world.

Top Shelf Drinks (Domestic & Imported)

Whiskies including Jack Daniel’s (red and black labels), Johnnie Walker, Ballantine’s, and Chivas Regal. Vodkas including Smirnoff, Barton, Oso Negro, and Blat. Gins including Beefeater and Tanqueray. Brandies including Fundador, Presidente, Don Pedro. Rums including Bacardi, Captain Morgan, and Baraima. Exceptional wines from around the world.

24-Hour Room Service

A wide selection of options, including breakfast favorites, entrees and main courses, soups, salads, snacks and desserts, available 24 hours a day.

All Taxes And Gratuities

Peace of mind and no surprises when it comes to gratuities and taxes under the all-inclusive plan. The perfect way to budget your vacation!
